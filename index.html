Why EHRServer?

EHRServer solves a very specific challenge all clinical informations systems come across: to build a clinical data repository. Clinical data is complex, heterogeneous and highly hierarchic. These characteristics make it very difficult to develop repositories that: 1. are generic (can handle different types of data structures), 2. are maintainable, 3. are performant, 4. are scalable and 5. are integrable.

At CaboLabs, it took us years of research and development to create a solution that complies with all of those requirements, to save EHR/EMR/PHR software companies time and money trying to solve the same problem again and again, with partial solutions.

EHRServer can help you to start working on you app right away, focusing on the features you will offer to your clients, and reduce time to market. Install your own self-managed instance of the EHRServer or use EHRServer Cloud.
